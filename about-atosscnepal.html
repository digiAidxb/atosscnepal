Who We Are

Atossc Nepal Pvt. Ltd. has been operating in the Nepalese education consultancy sector since 2014. Over the years, we have earned a reputation as one of the most reliable, ethical and student-focused consultancies in the country.

Our approach has always been simple — guide students honestly, prepare them correctly, and focus on long-term success rather than shortcuts.

Legitimacy & Recognition

Registered Company

Atossc Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is a legally registered company operating under Nepalese law.

Recognized by Ministry of Education

We are recognized by the Ministry of Education, Nepal, and follow official guidelines and compliance requirements.

Transparent Operations

From documentation to visa guidance, we maintain transparency at every stage of the process.
