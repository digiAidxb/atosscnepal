Study in Australia from Nepal

Australia is one of the most preferred study destinations for Nepalese students due to its globally recognised education system, diverse course options, and post-study work opportunities. However, studying in Australia requires meeting specific academic, English language, and financial criteria.

For Nepalese students, eligibility to study in Australia is assessed holistically. Universities and immigration authorities generally review academic progression, English test scores such as IELTS or PTE Academic, financial sustainability, and the Genuine Student (GS) requirement.

Common Requirements for Nepalese Students

  • Relevant academic background aligned with the chosen course
  • English language proficiency (IELTS or PTE Academic)
  • Clear and explainable financial support for tuition and living costs
  • Consistent documentation and academic history

Requirements may vary by institution and individual profile. This is why Atossc Nepal begins every application with a detailed eligibility assessment instead of directly submitting applications.

Our role is to help students understand their readiness, identify gaps, and prepare strategically before applying to Australian universities or lodging a student visa application.
